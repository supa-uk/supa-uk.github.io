Software Carpentry courses

The SUPA ‘Software Carpentry’ course (SUPASWC) includes versions of the ‘computing for scientists’ courses curated by the Software Carpentry organisation. Our variants of these texts are:

  • The Unix shell
  • Version control with Mercurial
  • Automation and Make
